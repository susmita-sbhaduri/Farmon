FarmOn Smart Farming
Highlights

An integrated application for managing your agricultural farm, track and plan your daily activities and obtain reports. Can be integrated with sensor data send from IOT devices.

Inventory and stocks

Manage your materials and goods from acquisition to application in your farm and check real time stocks.

Plan and Track

Plan your work in schedule calendar with automated application of Labor and Materials at the same time.

Human Resource

Track your employee salaries, loans, leaves and other personal details.

IOT Support

This app is scalable to get integrated to your IOT sensors which can send data to this app's API.

Extensive Reports

Comes with an extensive reporting mechanism of all modules like materials, goods, labor, HR etc.

Finance Tracking

Tracks and reports your expenses on materials, goods and labor as well incomes from farm produces.

Try in Mobile A responsive design to fit in your mobile device and work efficiently.
